About Our Pet Shop

Our Pet Shop was founded in 1990 with a mission to provide the highest quality care for pets, from puppies and kittens to birds and reptiles. Over the years, we have grown into a full-service pet shop, offering a wide range of services including grooming, training, and veterinary care. We are proud to serve our community and are committed to continuing our tradition of excellence in pet care.
